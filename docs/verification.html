Verification & Record Authority

PDF-Ready products may include verifiable records using cryptographic hashes, proof objects, or on-chain references.

Verification Components

  • SHA-256 hashes
  • Proof objects (.proof)
  • Append-only record index
  • Machine-readable validation scripts

Purpose

To ensure:
  • Authenticity
  • Integrity
  • Non-repudiation

Use

Customers may use provided verification tools to confirm that files have not been altered and originate from PDF-Ready.

Disclaimer

Verification systems provide technical assurance but do not constitute legal registration or government authority.
